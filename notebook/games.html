About me

I am an independent games developer in the U.S. Northeast, and work primarily in Common Lisp. Since 2008 I have worked on several projects for the PC platform. All my games are available for free and are compatible with Microsoft Windows, Mac OSX, and GNU/Linux. You can catch the latest news on my game development blog.
