The 2010 Lisp Game Design Challenge

Official mascot: Say hello to Biclops!

Challenge Instructions:

  1. announce your entry and starting time on the Challenge's cliki page.
  2. design, create, and release a game in 7 days using any dialect of LISP, between 18 March and 1 April, 2010. (Choose your start time, and release your completed entry within 7 days.)

Or, be a spectator and play/discuss the games!

Join our mailing list and/or IRC channel, irc.freenode.net#lispgames to get involved.
